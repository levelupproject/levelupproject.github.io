LEVEL UP! A Pixel Project based in Baltimore
Welcome to Level Up , a project started by Rizzo D aimed at bringing the adventure out in all of us. Each image below was shot in the Baltimore area during November and December of 2013 and then drawn over using Photoshop CS6. Each “pixel portrait” aims to embrace a childlike imagination and open up the mind of the viewer to the possibilities that surround them.
